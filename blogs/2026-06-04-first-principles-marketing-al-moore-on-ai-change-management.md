In this 10 Minute Martech episode from June 4, 2026, Al Moore, VP of Marketing at Fluency, discusses how successful AI implementation depends on reinforcing marketing fundamentals rather than pursuing automation alone. He emphasizes that the best technologies in martech have a democratizing effect, and that AI should be treated as a workforce multiplier requiring governance and cross-functional collaboration. Moore argues that organizations must move beyond isolated AI experimentation toward coordinated, company-wide approaches aligned with shared business objectives and proper data governance.
